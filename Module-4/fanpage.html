Bio

Sabaton are a Swedish metal band formed in 1999, with song topics focusing on battles or wars of historical significance. Throughout this time, the band has had multiple lineup changes, some due to personal circumstance and some due to creative reasons, the most significant being the departure of all members aside from the bass guitarist and vocalist in 2012.

The group focuses on the metal subgenres of "Power Metal" and "Heavy Metal", and have many songs that have both English and Swedish lyrical variations. The band is well known for their songs based in World War 2, which much of their discography contains. The band have also been featured by "Paradox Interactive", through 2 music DLC packs for the game "Hearts of Iron IV", which is a Real Time Strategy game based in WW2.

In recent times, the band has also partnered with Youtube historian Indy Niedell, known for his channel "The Great War", which followed World War 1 events as they happened, 100 years later, to explain in greater detail the history behind the battles that are written about in their songs
